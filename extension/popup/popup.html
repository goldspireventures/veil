      How it works

      1. Highlight a secret on any page
      2. Press Ctrl+Shift+S or click Secure
      3. Text becomes [redacted] — send as normal
      4. Recipient clicks [redacted] and enters the passphrase to unlock

      Quick start

      1. Paste or highlight a secret in Outlook or Gmail compose
      2. Choose Encrypt, Mask, or Tokenize from the Veil bar
      3. Send as normal — recipients click [redacted] or [veil:vt_…] to reveal

      Tokenize across email clients

      Tokenize works when sending from Outlook to Gmail (and vice versa). Each browser needs Veil installed and your team joined. Use the same team passphrase.

      Team setup

      Your team passphrase syncs when you join. IT-managed installs receive it automatically via browser policy.

      Keyboard shortcuts

      Ctrl+Shift+S secure · Ctrl+Shift+U unlock · Ctrl+Shift+G generate password

      Privacy

      Encryption runs in your browser. Secured content is not sent to our servers. Team portal & policies
