    How it works

    1. Highlight a secret on any page
    2. Press Ctrl+Shift+S or click Secure
    3. Text becomes [redacted] — send as normal
    4. Recipient clicks [redacted] and enters your passphrase to unlock

    How it works (team)

    1. Join your organization (join code, sign-in, or IT-managed policy)
    2. Highlight a secret → Ctrl+Shift+S
    3. Send the message — secret shows as [redacted]
    4. Teammate clicks [redacted] → one-click or passphrase prompt → secret appears

    Organization provisioning

    Team passphrases are pushed by IT policy (managed browsers) or Goldspire cloud (join code / SSO). When IT rotates secrets, your extension updates automatically — no manual entry.

    External vault mode is optional for teams that keep the passphrase only in a password manager.

    Security

    AES-256-GCM + PBKDF2 (600k iterations). All encryption runs in your browser. No secrets leave your device.
